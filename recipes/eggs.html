Creamy Scrambled Eggs: Gordon Ramsay's Recipe

How to make the eggs

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 15g Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Creme Fraiche(Sour Cream)
  • Chives

Steps

  1. In a deep saucepan, crack 6 cold eggs. To make smaller batches, use a 2-to-1 egg-to-butter ratio.
  2. Put the pan on high heat, and add the butter.
  3. Stir and turn the eggs constantly with a rubber spatula until they start to thicken, scraping the bottom of the pan as you go.
  4. After 30 seconds, take the pan off the heat but keep stirring. Put the heat back on after about 10 seconds. Repeat this process for 3 minutes.
  5. Remove the eggs from the heat at the last minute and season lightly with salt and pepper. For an extra creamy texture, stir in 1 tbsp of creme fraiche (sour cream).
  6. Serve with chopped chives as a garnish.
